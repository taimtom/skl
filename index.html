About Us

At Skill Land Academy we are committed to solving the problem of unemployment which is caused by of lack necessary skill-set required to get employed. So we offer high-in-demand tech courses aimed at teaching neccessary skills required for students to land their dream job in the tech industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce unemployment in Africa to the barest minimum

We Achieve this by:

  • Trainning students in highly demanded skills.
  • Mentoring them closely to ease problem solving.
  • Consistently monitoring to increase completion rate.
  • Job search mentoring to foster employment.
